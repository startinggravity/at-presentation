  • Where do you sleep?
Where do you sleep?
A series of shelters, spaced roughly 8-10 miles apart, are placed along the trail.
Some shelters are grungy. Some are nice. They often have space nearby for tent camping.
Many nights I sleep in my own tent.
In the tent I have a sleeping bag and a quilt, which together only weigh 2 lbs.
  • What do you eat?
What do you eat?
Some food is specially prepared for backpackers.
A lot of times backpackers shop for food at grocery stores. If you look for the right kinds of food you can find plenty to eat.
I carry a small stove when I want a hot meal.
